EXCELLENCE IN CLUB MANAGEMENT AWARDS PANEL

The National Club Association is excited to host the Excellence in Club Management Awards Panel at this year’s National Club Conference. Winners from the 2019 awards will discuss the Private Club Industry and keys to success from their respective clubs. The Excellence in Club Management Awards recognize private club general managers and chief operating officers who have exhibited outstanding skills in managing their club. It is co-sponsored by McMahon Group, Club & Resort Business Magazine and the National Club Association. More Information will be available as the winners of the awards are announced! Winners will be announced January 2020

Lodging at the Ocean Reef Club

Ocean Reef Club and Resort offers a “unique way of life.” Located on a private, pristine peninsula beside America's only living reef, ORC is bounded on three sides by water and on the fourth side by federal and state lands. It is located on the northernmost tip of Key Largo and is accessible by land, sea or air.

Usually reserved for members, Ocean Reef Club offers a rare opportunity for National Club Conference attendees to experience the world’s largest private club at this exclusive island destination. Located just an hour south of Miami on the island of Key Largo, the intimate oasis of ORC awaits with its tropical abundance of things to do. Its rare blend of natural beauty and fine-hewn luxury lets work flow easily into play.
